product

## Users

This product serves CNC quality inspection teams working across factory-floor and management contexts.

Primary users:

- Inspectors use mobile phones and tablets on the factory floor to enter measurements quickly, confirm pass/fail status, upload defect photos, and keep inspection work moving without paper forms.
- Managers monitor quality performance, defect trends, SPC signals, reports, and master data from larger screens while still needing reliable tablet access.
- Admins manage users, roles, factories, machines, product models, inspection criteria, and operational access.

The interface must support Korean and Vietnamese users. UI copy, validation messages, navigation, and status labels should remain translatable and readable in both languages.

## Product Purpose

The CNC Quality Inspection KPI App digitizes manual CNC inspection workflows, reduces missing or incorrect records, and turns inspection history into usable operational data.

The product exists to help teams:

- Replace paper inspection sheets with reliable digital capture.
- Validate measured values against inspection specs in real time.
- Register defects immediately with supporting photos and status tracking.
- Monitor defect rate, FPY, inspection volume, inspection time, and SPC signals.
- Generate reports and support continuous process improvement from inspection data.

Success means inspectors can complete measurements with minimal friction on mobile devices, managers can see quality problems early, and the organization can trust the inspection data.

## Brand Personality

The product personality is:

- Field-friendly
- Clear
- Robust

The tone should feel practical, direct, and operational. It should prioritize confidence under real factory conditions over decorative polish. The UI should make the next action obvious, keep critical states visible, and avoid ambiguity around pass/fail, defects, alerts, and permissions.

## Anti-references

This should not look or behave like a flashy SaaS landing page. Avoid marketing-style hero sections, decorative gradients, oversized promotional copy, animated spectacle, and visuals that compete with inspection work.

Avoid:

- Interfaces that feel designed for sales demos rather than repeated factory use.
- Dense tables or small controls that are difficult to operate on mobile or tablet screens.
- Excessive card grids that fragment operational information.
- Decorative motion, visual noise, or styling that slows down scanning.
- Dark neon dashboard aesthetics unless a specific workflow later proves they are necessary.
- Hardcoded UI text that bypasses Korean and Vietnamese translation support.

## Design Principles

1. Mobile inspection first. The primary workflow must work well on factory-floor phones and tablets before desktop enhancements are considered.
2. Make status unmistakable. Pass/fail, defect state, inspection progress, sync state, and permission boundaries should be instantly clear.
3. Reduce input friction. Inspectors should spend time measuring, not navigating. Forms should be short, forgiving, and optimized for touch.
4. Preserve operational trust. Data entry, validation, reports, and KPI views should feel stable, auditable, and predictable.
5. Serve different roles without bloating the UI. Inspectors, managers, and admins need different task density and navigation, but the product should feel like one coherent tool.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Mobile and tablet usability is a core accessibility requirement. Touch targets should be large enough for factory-floor use, text should remain legible in Korean and Vietnamese, and layouts should handle longer translated labels without clipping.

Design and implementation should prioritize:

- WCAG AA contrast as the baseline.
- Color-blind-safe treatment for pass/fail and alert states, using text, icons, and shape in addition to color.
- Reduced reliance on motion, with no critical information conveyed only through animation.
- Reliable operation on tablets and modest factory-floor devices.
- Clear focus states and keyboard-accessible controls for management and admin workflows.
